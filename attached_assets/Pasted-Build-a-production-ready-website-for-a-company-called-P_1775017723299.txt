STRATEGIC CONTENT RULES (CRITICAL)
-----------------------------------

This website must NOT feel like a generic IT services or SaaS company.

Ensure all content follows these principles:

1. Problem-first messaging:
Each section must describe real-world problems such as:
- fragmented workflows
- manual operations
- disconnected systems
- underutilized data
- lack of decision visibility

2. Positioning:
PHAARVAI must feel like:
- a digital systems company
- an AI implementation partner
- a mission-driven innovation firm
- NOT a generic software agency

3. Language rules:
Avoid words like:
- cutting-edge
- revolutionary
- disruptive
- world-class

Use language like:
- practical AI deployment
- scalable systems
- operational intelligence
- institutional capability
- measurable impact

4. Differentiation:
Clearly show:
- strategy + execution in one
- built for scale, not pilots
- designed for real-world institutional use

5. Funding alignment:
Content must reflect that PHAARVAI supports:
- funded programs
- CSR initiatives
- donor-backed projects
- public-impact systems

But DO NOT say:
“we help you get grants”

Instead say:
“we support implementation-ready, fundable initiatives”
